Common Questions

Before you reach out.

Is the discovery call really free?
Yes. It is a 30-minute call to understand your business and see if we are the right fit. No pitch, no obligation, no invoice.
Do I need to be in Belize to work with you?
No. Canopy Consulting is remote-first. We deliver all services via video calls, shared documents, and cloud tools. We work with clients anywhere.
What if I am not sure which phase I need?
That is exactly what the Business Audit is for. We assess where you are, identify what is missing, and tell you clearly what you need first.
How long do engagements take?
Phase 1 and 2 packages typically take 3 to 6 weeks. The Business Audit takes 1 to 2 weeks. Retainer engagements are ongoing monthly.
Can I start with one service and expand later?
Absolutely. Most clients start with a Business Audit, then move into Phase 1 or 2. The journey is designed to be taken one step at a time.
Is the audit fee applied to my project?
Yes. If you move forward with any package after the audit, the audit fee is fully credited toward that engagement.
